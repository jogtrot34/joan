Who We Are

Crafting Quality Lifestyle Solutions

Joan Investments is a proudly Malawian company providing exceptional lifestyle solutions — from handcrafted leather products to professional dry cleaning and elegant wedding car hire.

We are committed to empowering women, youth, and people with disabilities through meaningful employment. Every product and service reflects our dedication to authenticity, quality, and community impact.

Authentic Handcrafted Sustainable Malawian Inclusive

Our Vision

To become Malawi's leading provider of lifestyle solutions, renowned for handcrafted quality, exceptional service, and positive community impact across Blantyre, Lilongwe, Mzuzu, and beyond.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, customer-focused lifestyle solutions while empowering marginalized groups through sustainable employment, training, and economic opportunities.
