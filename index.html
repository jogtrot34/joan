Who We Are

Crafting Quality Lifestyle Solutions

Joan Investments is a Malawian company that provides high-quality lifestyle solutions. We empower women, youth, and people with disabilities by offering them meaningful jobs. We specialize in handcrafted real leather products including shoes, bags, belts, and wallets, professional dry cleaning and laundry, and elegant wedding car hire with chauffeurs.

We focus on authenticity, quality, and sustainability in everything we do.

Authentic Handcrafted Sustainable Malawian Inclusive

Our Vision

To become Malawi's leading provider of lifestyle solutions, known for handcrafted quality, excellent service, and positive community impact.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, customer-focused lifestyle solutions while empowering women, youth, and people with disabilities.
